Our Story

Founded in 2015, the Green Thumb Community Garden has grown from a small neighborhood project to a thriving green space that serves hundreds of local residents.

Our Mission

We aim to promote sustainable gardening practices, provide fresh produce to the community, and create a space for education and wellness.
