NP Membership Benefits

Our Non-Profit Membership is designed for charitable organizations and community groups:

  • Free Directory Listing: Get listed in our business directory
  • Community Events: Access to all chamber community events
  • Monthly Newsletter: Stay informed with our digital newsletter
  • Resource Library: Access to basic business resources
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with other non-profits and businesses

This membership level has no fee but requires annual renewal and verification of non-profit status.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze Membership is perfect for small businesses and startups:

  • All NP Benefits: Plus additional features
  • Training Workshops: Discounted access to business training
  • Social Media Promotion: Featured in our social media channels
  • Event Discounts: 15% off chamber events
  • Business Resources: Access to premium business tools
  • Referral Network: Connect with other bronze members

  • Leadership Opportunities: Serve on chamber committees
  • Exclusive Networking: Access to invitation-only gatherings
  • Annual Spotlight: Dedicated annual feature
